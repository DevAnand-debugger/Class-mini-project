## 📌 Aim  
To perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on a retail sales dataset and apply time series techniques to analyze trends and forecast future sales.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Libraries Used  
- Python  
- Pandas  
- NumPy  
- Matplotlib  
- Seaborn  
- Scikit-learn  

---

## 📊 Dataset Description  
The dataset contains retail transaction data with the following features:
- Transaction ID  
- Date  
- Customer ID  
- Gender  
- Age  
- Product Category  
- Quantity  
- Price per Unit  
- Total Amount  

👉 The dataset is used to analyze sales patterns, customer behavior, and revenue trends.

---

## ⚙️ Steps Performed  

### 1. Data Cleaning  
- Converted Date column to datetime format  
- Removed duplicate entries  
- Checked for missing values  

👉 Insight: Clean data ensures accurate analysis.

---

### 2. Feature Engineering  
- Extracted Month, Day, Year from Date  
- Created Age Groups (Young, Adult, Senior)  
- Calculated Price per Item  

👉 Insight: New features help in better understanding patterns.

---

## 📊 Visualizations Implemented  

### 1. Bar Chart (Sales by Product Category)  
- Shows total sales for each category  

👉 Insight: Identifies highest and lowest performing categories.

---

### 2. Pie Chart (Category-wise Distribution)  
- Displays percentage contribution of each category  

👉 Insight: Helps understand revenue share.

---

### 3. Daily Sales Trend (Time Series)  
- Shows variation of sales over time  

👉 Insight: Reveals fluctuations and demand patterns.

---

### 4. Moving Average (Smoothed Trend)  
- Reduces noise in daily sales data  

👉 Insight: Helps identify overall trend clearly.

---

### 5. Monthly Sales Trend  
- Aggregates sales by month  

👉 Insight: Shows seasonal patterns and peak months.

---

### 6. Histogram (Sales Distribution)  
- Shows frequency of sales values  

👉 Insight: Most transactions fall within a specific range.

---

### 7. Boxplot (Outlier Detection)  
- Identifies extreme values in sales  

👉 Insight: Detects unusually high transactions.

---

### 8. Scatter Plot (Quantity vs Total Amount)  
- Shows relationship between quantity and sales  

👉 Insight: Higher quantity leads to higher revenue.

---

### 9. Heatmap (Correlation Matrix)  
- Shows relationships between numerical features  

👉 Insight:  
- Strong correlation between Price and Total Amount  
- Moderate correlation between Quantity and Sales  

---

## 🔮 Time Series Forecasting  

- Applied Linear Regression model  
- Converted time into numerical format  
- Predicted next 7 days of sales  

👉 Insight: Helps estimate future sales trends.

---

## 📈 Conclusion  
This project demonstrates how EDA and time series analysis can be used to:  
- Understand sales patterns  
- Identify customer behavior  
- Analyze trends over time  
- Predict future sales  

These insights can help businesses make data-driven decisions.
